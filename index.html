About

Hello there!

Over a decade ago, I bought my first domain name and started tinkering with the web. Since then, I've designed for Nomic and coded for Mozilla and Instagram, won international contests run by Google and the Bugzilla project, and shared a bit of the web craft with 40,000+ students at Codecademy.

I currently study computer science at the Tufts School of Engineering in Medford, MA.

To get in touch, @mention me on Twitter. To provide feedback about a project of mine, please file an issue on Github.
