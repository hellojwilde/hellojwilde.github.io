About

Hello there!

Over a decade ago, I bought my first domain name and started tinkering with the web. Since then, I've designed and coded for Nomic and Mozilla, won international contests run by Google and the Bugzilla project, and shared a bit of the web craft with 40,000+ students at Codecademy.

I currently study at the Tufts School of Engineering in Medford, MA, majoring in computer science and minoring in communications and media studies.

On the side, I'm developing an interactive workbook platform at Tufts CEEO, contributing to InnCrisis, and working on a LocalWiki-based project.

To get in touch, @mention me on Twitter. To provide feedback about a project of mine, please file an issue on Github.
